Smoked Chicken Wings

This is an easy, can't miss technique for smoking chicken wings. No smoker needed - all you need is a Weber Kettle Charcoal Grill

Ingredients

  • 2 dozen chicken wings - sectioned
  • Laura's Wing Rub

Steps

  1. Apply rub liberally to wings
  2. Set up kettle for smoking (snake method)
  3. Start 15 briquettes in charcoal starter. Let them ash over
  4. Dump ashed over briquettes at beginning of snake
  5. Replace grill back to kettle and cover
  6. Once up to temp (225 degrees), add wings to grill. Keep wings as far away from snake as possible
  7. Smoke for 2 hours, check on wings and charcoal supply. If they are not too dark, smoke for 30 more minutes
  8. Remove wings from grill and put in bowl and coat the wings with sauce. Let them sit for 1 hour to 1 day in the refrigerator
  9. Grill wings on gas grill until hot and desired crispiness, 10 minutes or more
  10. Enjoy
